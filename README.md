# 🎨 Color Hunt

**The ultimate Stroop Effect challenge.** Focus on the ink color, ignore the word!

Color Hunt is a fast-paced, high-focus web game designed to test your cognitive processing speed. Based on the famous [Stroop Effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stroop_effect), it challenges your brain to differentiate between a written word and its actual color.

---

## 🚀 Features

-   **⚡ Fast-Paced Gameplay**: 30 seconds to score as much as possible.
-   **🔥 Combo System**: Rack up correct answers in a row to multiply your score!
-   **🔊 Dynamic Audio**: Procedurally generated sound effects that increase in pitch as your combo grows.
-   **✨ Visual Feedback**: Particle explosions, screen shakes, and color flashes for an immersive experience.
-   **📈 Balanced Difficulty**: The timer gets tighter as you score higher.
-   **🏆 Persistent High Scores**: Your best scores are saved automatically in your browser.
-   **📱 Responsive & Premium Design**: Glassmorphism UI with smooth animations and a professional color palette.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1.  **Look at the word** in the center of the screen.
2.  **Identify the color** it is printed in (ignore what the word actually says!).
3.  **Click the matching color button** in the grid below.
4.  **Don't run out of time!** Correct answers give you a small time boost.
